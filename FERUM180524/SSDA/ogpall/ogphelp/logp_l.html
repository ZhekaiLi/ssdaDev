logp_l

Purpose

Computes the log-predictive probability for Gaussian noise

Synopsis 

[allLog] = logp_l(likpar,y,mX,sigX2);

Description

[allLog] = logp_l(likpar,y,mX,sigX2) - returns the sum of log-predictive probabilities of the inputs -- assuming additive Gaussian noise.

It assumes that the test samples are independent, thus ignoring any possible non-diagonal part in the joint posterior distribution.

Parameters:

likpar

- the likelihood parameters.

y

- desired output.

mx

- mean of the GP marginal at the new input.

allLog

- the average error.
